# Sweet & Sour Sauce

### Ingredients

- sugar (1/2 cup)
- Ketchup (4tbsp)
- Vinegar (1/2 cup)
- Soy sauce (1 tbsp)
- Garlic powder (1 tsp)
- Hot sauce (1/2 tsp)

### Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together in a bowl
2. Add to whatever meat you're cooking
